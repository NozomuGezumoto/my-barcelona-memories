# Privacy Policy  
My Barcelona Memories  

Last updated: February 2026  

My Barcelona Memories (“the App”) respects your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration.

We do not collect, store, or share personal data such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Payment information

All memories, notes, and pinned locations are stored locally on your device unless otherwise specified.

## 2. Location Data

The App may use your device location to help you place pins on the map accurately.

Location data:
- Is used only within the app
- Is not stored on external servers
- Is not shared with third parties

You can disable location access anytime in your device settings.

## 3. Analytics

The App may use basic analytics tools provided by the app store platforms to understand download numbers and general usage trends.

No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

## 4. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites (for example, places in Barcelona).  
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

## 5. Data Security

All saved memories remain on your device.  
We do not operate external servers to store user content.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.  
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
